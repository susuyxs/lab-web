About

The Brain and Intelligence Center, affiliated with Chongqing University of Technology and led by Prof. JunRui Zhang, is dedicated to cutting-edge research and technological innovation in brain-computer interfaces and biomedical engineering.
Our team includes outstanding PhDs and engineers who graduated from renowned institutions such as EPFL, CUHK, Fudan University, Harbin Institute of Technology, UESTC, and SUSTech, with expertise spanning micro/nano electronics, integrated circuits, artificial intelligence, biosensing, and chemical materials.

Research

BCI Core Module (EEG Acquisition + Electrical Stimulation)

This compact BCI module integrates EEG signal acquisition and electrical stimulation therapy, with a small form factor and high adaptability for integration into various rehabilitation or research devices.

Wireless Methanethiol Sensing Capsule (Electrochemical Detection + Real-time Wireless Transmission)

This capsule system combines a high-sensitivity electrochemical front-end with low-power Bluetooth communication, designed for in situ monitoring and analysis of methanethiol gas in the gastrointestinal tract.

Flexible Acquisition Patch

This patch integrates a high-density flexible electrode array and liquid metal circuitry for surface EMG signal acquisition, featuring excellent stretchability, fit, and high SNR. It enables real-time monitoring of muscle load, fatigue, and tendon displacement for injury prevention, and can be used in gesture recognition, prosthesis control, and VR/AR motion capture.

Integrated Vascular Stent Electrode

Utilizing multi-process synergy, this innovation fuses electrodes and stents for high-performance neurointerventional devices, advancing clinical precision diagnostics and treatment.
