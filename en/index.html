About

We focus on brain-computer interfaces and neural engineering: neural signal acquisition and decoding, closed-loop stimulation, wearable and clinical translation.

Mission

Make neurotechnology more reliable, usable, and interpretable.

Focus

BCI decoding, neurostimulation, neuromodulation, and systems engineering.

Open Science

Reproducible pipeline, data and tool sharing.

Research

Neural Decoding

EEG/iEEG feature extraction, time-frequency analysis, deep learning, and interpretability.

Closed-loop Stimulation

Constant current stimulation, parameter optimization, trigger logic, and safety design.

Wearable Systems

Low-power acquisition, wireless transmission, real-time inference, and edge computing.

Clinical Translation

Research paradigms, ethics compliance, usability, and engineering implementation.
