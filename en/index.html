About

The Brain & Intelligence Center at Chongqing University of Technology focuses on brain–computer interface technologies and intelligent human–machine interaction.

Research Directions

Brain–computer interface research on neural signal decoding and intelligent interaction.

Technology & Platform

Integrating neural signal acquisition, AI algorithms, and real-time BCI systems.

Applications & Collaboration
